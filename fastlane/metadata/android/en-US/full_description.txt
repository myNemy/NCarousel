NCarousel downloads images from a folder on your Nextcloud server (via WebDAV) and sets them as your Android wallpaper.

Features:
- Connect to your own Nextcloud instance
- Pick a remote folder (e.g. Photos)
- Manual "next wallpaper" action (and Quick Settings tile, if enabled)
- Automatic wallpaper rotation with a minimum interval of 1 minute (WorkManager one-shot chaining)
- Optional on-device cache to reduce downloads

Privacy:
- No trackers
- Credentials are stored encrypted on-device (AndroidX Security)
- Network connections only go to the Nextcloud server you configure
